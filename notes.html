V1.02 Notes

- Fixed Ping Pong mode so that it accurately jumps from left to right instead of slowly summing to mono. - Width is now wider and goes up to 1000 samples of delay. - The mod now goes to 4 Hz, and uses a new more intensive algorithm. It now should sound less harsh. - Fixed shading on buttons when hovered over. - Peaking filters now are wide Q. - HPF and LPF now go from 20 - 10000 Hz, it felt pointless for them to go higher. - Feedback can now go up to 1.0, which is an infinite delay. - Gain rescaled to go to 8.0, unity is now 4.0.
